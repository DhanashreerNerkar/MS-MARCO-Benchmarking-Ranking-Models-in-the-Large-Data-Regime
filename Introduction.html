INTRODUCTION

The Evolution of Information Retrieval in the Large-Data Era

The MS MARCO Journey

2016
MS MARCO Birth
MS MARCO series of datasets released, aiming to help academic researchers explore information access in the large-data regime. First dataset designed to support neural IR research with massive supervised training data.
2016-2018
Multi-Task Adoption
Datasets used across various tasks: keyphrase extraction, question answering, and text ranking. Passage ranking and document ranking tasks receive the most attention, associated with competitive leaderboards.
2018-2019
Neural IR Renaissance
Before MS MARCO, neural methods were benchmarked on proprietary, non-English, or synthetic datasets. MS MARCO enabled fair comparison between emerging neural methods and traditional IR approaches.
January 2019
BERT Breakthrough
Nogueira and Cho's work represents the first application of pretrained transformers to ranking tasks - a watershed moment that ushered in a new era of research dominated by pretrained transformer models.
